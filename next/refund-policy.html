Refund & Cancellation Policy

At DHYANSHIV INDIA PRIVATE LIMITED, we strive to provide exceptional IT and compliance services. This Refund & Cancellation Policy explains how we handle requests for cancellations, refunds, and payment reversals.

Service eligibility

Services purchased through our website are subject to review and approval. Once payment is captured through Razorpay, service delivery begins immediately unless otherwise agreed.

Cancellation requests

Cancellation requests must be submitted in writing to support@dhyanshivindia.in. We will evaluate each request based on service stage, work completed, and contractual obligations.

Refund policy

Refunds are considered on a case-by-case basis. If a refund is approved, it may be issued through Razorpay in accordance with their settlement timelines and the specific service agreement.

Service delivery and exceptions

Refunds are not guaranteed. Services that have already begun or that require non-recoverable preparation may be partially or fully non-refundable.

Contact information

If you have questions about your purchase, cancellation, or refund, please contact our support team at support@dhyanshivindia.in. We will respond promptly and professionally.
